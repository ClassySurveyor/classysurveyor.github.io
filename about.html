About

I am a class surveyor for a classification society working with new-building projects and in-service ships and offshore units. I also carry out surveys for certification of materials and components as well as other third-party verification services.

The purpose of this blog is to share some of the experiences and the knowledge that I am exposed to. For now I am starting it off as simply a blog and we shall see where it goes as time passes.

Thanks for visiting and I hope you enjoy the materials!
